## <a id="Children_Chronic_Conditions_CCC"></a><a id="_Toc400546171"></a><a id="_Toc74826756"></a><a id="_Toc171403039"></a><a id="CCC"></a>Children With Chronic Conditions \(CCC\)

This measure is collected using survey methodology\. Refer to *HEDIS Volume 3: Specifications for Survey Measures* for the measure specifications and data collection protocols\.

Description

This measure provides information on parents’ experience with their child’s Medicaid organization for the population of children with chronic conditions\. Three composites summarize satisfaction with basic components of care essential for successful treatment, management and support of children with chronic conditions:

1. Access to Specialized Services\.
2. Family Centered Care: Personal Doctor Who Knows Child\.
3. Coordination of Care for Children With Chronic Conditions\.

Item\-specific question summary rates are reported for each composite question\. Question summary rates are also reported individually for two items summarizing the following concepts:

1. Access to Prescription Medicines\.
2. Family Centered Care: Getting Needed Information\.
